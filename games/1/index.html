Instruction
You are steering a ship and your job is to dispose of all the rubbish lying around the map. Be weary though because due to all the illegal dumping people did, the rubbish got angry and are now coming for you. Once the hordes of rubbish reach your height the game will be over and the island will be doomed.
Rules
Different kind of rubbish can only be destroyed
with their according kind of laser that is:
plastic - yellow:
paper - blue
glass - green
The darker the rubbish is the more hits
it will take to destroy it (up to 3)
If the rubbish reach ship level game will be over.
Steering
- Press ARROWS to move left and right
- Press SPACE to shoot
- Press 1, 2 or 3 to change laser type
- Press R to cool down your weapon
Enemies
The additional threats will also appear
the harder the difficulty the more enemies
that will try to destroy you will be spawned
by the rubbish. Once they come in contact
with the ship the game will be over.
